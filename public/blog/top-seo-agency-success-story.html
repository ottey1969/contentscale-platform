The 90-Day Recovery Strategy

We developed a three-phase approach focused on transforming their content from "SEO-optimized garbage" (their words, not ours) into genuinely helpful, AI-era content.

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

  1. Content Audit: Identify top 15 money pages
  2. Competitor Analysis: Scan top 3 competitors using ContentScore
  3. Framework Implementation: Train team on GRAAF + CRAFT
  4. Quick Wins: Update 5 highest-traffic pages first

Phase 2: Execution (Days 31-60)

  1. Rewrite Priority Content: 15 core service pages
  2. Add Credibility Elements: Case studies, author bios, certifications
  3. Improve Actionability: Clear CTAs, next steps, free tools
  4. Optimize for CRAFT: Better flow, audience-appropriate tone

  4. Distribution: LinkedIn, email, partnerships
